About Mera

Welcome to Mera Clothing, a brand proudly made in the Philippines. At Mera, we believe in creating stylish, comfortable, and affordable clothing that fits seamlessly into your everyday life. Our collection of basic tops and body-hugging designs is crafted with care and precision, ensuring that each piece not only looks great but also feels great to wear.We provide high-quality, stylish clothing that is perfect for everyday use. We are dedicated to creating pieces that are both fashionable and functional, allowing you to look and feel your best every day.

Cotton

At Mera, the organic cotton we use is grown by farmer's associations in the Philippines who cultivate it with respect for both people and the environment. Since the launch of the Mera project in 2022, we have sourced a significant amount of this sustainable cotton directly from local producer associations located in various regions across the Philippines.

Upcycling

Upcycling consists in recycling materials or products that have fallen out of use in order to turn them into higher quality or more useful products.
